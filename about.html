About SCU TKE

The Upsilon Omega Chapter

  • Founded on November 1, 2014
  • 64 current active members on campus.
  • Many local alumni working in the Bay Area.
  • Heavily involved in Associated Student Government, Into the Wild, and other on-campus organizations.

Tau Kappa Epsilon

  • Founded on January 10, 1899 at Illinois Wesleyan University in Bloomington, Illinois.
  • 270,000 members as of January 2016.
  • Over 250 active chapters.
  • 30 full-time employees on the professional Fraternity Staff. Thousands volunteer as unpaid staff.
  • Tau Kappa Epsilon publishes a quarterly magazine titled The Teke

Activities and Events

Philanthropy

As part of the plan to be better men for a better world, the Upsilon Omega chapter strives to host and attend many philanthropy events. Recently, the chapter had an event in which they partnered up with Camp Kesem and wrote letters to raise money for children whose families have been affected by cancer. The Upsilon Omega Chapter also work with other organizations including St. Jude's Childrens Hospital, Stand Up for Kids Homeless Shelter, and the Second Harvest Food Bank.

Mixers

Mixers happen very often throughout every quarter. These events are a great chance for members of the fraternity to socialize and get to know some members of every sorority.

Brotherhood Events

Brotherhood is the bond that keeps the fraternity; therefore, a couple times a quarter we hold events that include going hiking, camping, to a sports game, golfing, or trips together.

Red Carnation Ball

Red Carnation Ball is our formal event that happens towards the end of every spring quarter. This weekend long formal event is a fun time where everyone attends and some of the best college memories are made.

Conferences

We make a commitment to attending the regional leadership conferences and conclaves that Tau Kappa Epsilon holds. It is a great chance to network with fraters from other chapters and learn some new things.

Sports

Our brothers make a commitment to create many teams in many different sports in the school-wide intramural league.
